Plain Dosa Recipe

Description

Plain Dosa is a classic South Indian dish that needs no introduction. It is a thin, crispy pancake with fermented rice and lentil batter, served with various chutneys and sambar. The humble Plain Dosa is a staple in many Indian households and is loved for its simplicity and versatility. Whether you have it for breakfast, lunch or dinner, itâ€™s a dish that will satisfy your taste buds.

Ingredients

  • 4 cup Boiled Rice
  • 1 1/4 cup Urad Dal
  • 1 tsp Fenugreek Seeds
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • Salt - as per taste

Steps

  1. Soak rice and urad dal separately for 3 hours.
  2. Drain and grind them separately and smoothly.
  3. Mix both the batter together along with salt and close with a lid.
  4. Allow it to ferment for 5 to 6 hours.
  5. Heat a tawa and smear it with 1 tsp of oil in the center.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the tawa.
  7. Spread it evenly into a round shape.
  8. Add 1 tsp of oil on the sides and on the surface of dosa.
  9. Turn over when the underside is done and cook the other side.
  10. Serve hot with chutney and sambar or with dosa molaga podi.
